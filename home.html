Our Services

Event Planning

Capture some of the magic of a historic castle by renting one for your event — our event planning team will help you make the time you spend at our locations as fantastic as we possibly can.

In-House Design Team

Working with the exteriors and interiors of our locations can appear to be a daunting, but with the assistance of our design teams, it doesn’t have to be. They’re a mixture of specialists in everything from lining up the perfect photo in our locations’ picturesque gardens, to knowing all the twists and turns of a castle’s corridors, and what will fit down them.

Links with Local Businesses and Transport Providers

Logistics can often be a problem, when you’re talking about castles in defensible positions, far from cities. We’ve partnered with a variety of local firms, so if you need coaches to help your guests arrive, catering for an event, or something else entirely, our staff will know who to call.
